# World Tree v0.5 Product Experience Rebuild

Date: 2026-07-10  
Branch: `codex/v0.5-product-experience-rebuild`  
Status: implementation and automated QA complete; human playtest and screen recording remain required.

## Outcome

World Tree now presents a coherent local-first product rather than an engineering console. The global navigation is limited to 首页、体验、我的内容、创作、设置. The eight canonical capabilities remain in `browser/app/product-registry.js` and pass the browser entry matrix; runtime aliases still do not create extra product entries.

The product language is “Living Archive”: dark forest navigation, warm paper content, reading-oriented serif headings, restrained gold attention color, visible keyboard focus, fixed product theme, reduced-motion support, and a five-item mobile navigation bar.
